Beansicle

Description

This is a recipe for a refreshing, scrumptious and cooling beansicle!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of red beans
  • 1 soul of prawn

Steps

  1. Empty red beans from tin into popsicle containers.
  2. Transfer soul of prawn into one lucky beansicle.
  3. Reap your rewards in about 5 hours
