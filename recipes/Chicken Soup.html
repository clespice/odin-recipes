Chicken Soup

Description

This is a recipe for a rustic, delicious and comforting bowl of chicken soup. Full of nutrients and good for the soul!

This recipe will take roughly 30 to 45 minutes to make and serves 2, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 large red onion
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 large stalk of celery
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 large chicken thigh
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 litre of chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut onions, carrot and celeries into bite sized pieces.
  2. Mince the cloves of garlic.
  3. Add butter and garlic to a pot on medium heat. Add in the rest of the vegetables when garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add in your chicken stock and chicken thigh, bring it to a boil and put a lid on it. Dont forget the bay leaves!
  5. Once chicken is fully cooked, the recipe is done! Shred the chicken, Season to taste and serve.
