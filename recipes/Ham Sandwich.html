Totally Normal Ham Sandwich

Description

A totally normal classic ham sandwich enjoyed by all ages. Get creative with it!

Ingredients

  • 2 loaves of bread
  • 1/2 slice of ham
  • 3 shreds of lettuce
  • 3 slices of tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tbsp motor oil

Steps

  1. Arrange your ham and vegetables onto the bread.
  2. Mix the mayo and motor oil, this is your secret sauce
  3. Assemble sandwich any way you like and enjoy!
